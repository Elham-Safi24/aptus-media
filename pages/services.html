FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to be present during the shoot? +
No — most agents just leave access and let us work. We handle the lighting, staging adjustments, and shooting order. If you want to be there, you're welcome, but it's not required.
How do I receive the photos? +
Via a branded online gallery link. You can download individual images or the full set as a ZIP. Files come in MLS-ready JPEG resolution and a separate web-optimized version.
What areas do you serve? +
Fort Worth, Dallas, and the full DFW metroplex — including Arlington, Keller, Southlake, Mansfield, Grapevine, and surrounding cities. Travel fees may apply beyond 40 miles.
What if the weather is bad on shoot day? +
We monitor weather closely before every shoot. For overcast or rainy conditions we'll either reschedule at no charge or proceed and use sky replacement in post — your call.
Do you offer packages for property managers or high-volume agents? +
Yes — agents and brokerages booking 5+ shoots per month get a custom rate. Contact us and we'll put together a retainer package that works for your volume.
