FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to be there during the shoot?+
No — most agents just leave access and let us work. We handle the shoot from start to finish. If you want to be there, you're welcome to.
How do I receive the photos?+
Via an online gallery link. You can download individual images or the full set as a ZIP. Files come MLS-ready at high resolution.
What areas do you serve?+
Fort Worth, Dallas, and the full DFW metroplex — including Arlington, Keller, Southlake, Mansfield, Grapevine, and surrounding cities.
What if the weather is bad?+
We monitor weather before every shoot. For bad conditions we'll either reschedule at no charge or proceed and use sky replacement in editing — your call.
